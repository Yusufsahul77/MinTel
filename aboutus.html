A Chief Executive Officer (CEO) is the highest-ranking executive in a company, responsible for making major corporate decisions, managing overall operations, and leading the organization toward its strategic goals. The role of a CEO is multifaceted, encompassing leadership, vision, strategic planning, and effective communication. A Chief Operating Officer (COO) is a key executive responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of a company, ensuring that the organization's business processes are efficient, effective, and aligned with its strategic objectives. A company's financial components are overseen by a top-level executive known as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO). The CFO is essential to the development of the organization's financial strategy, risk management, and general financial well-being.
